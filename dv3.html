Latitude vs Cloudiness

The correlation between cloudiness and distance from the equator is negligible.

There was some variation by hemisphere. In the Northern Hemisphere, the correlation is negligible (r < .2), while in the Southern Hemisphere it is low (.2 < r < .4)
